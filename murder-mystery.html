🔪 MURDER MYSTERY KIT — SAMPLE

Murder at Tidal Manor

A Beach House Whodunit for 6-8 Players

Someone didn't survive the weekend. One of you knows why. Duration: 2-3 hours. No costumes required.

🎭 Host Guide

Setup time: 15 minutes. Print character cards, hide clue envelopes in 4 locations around the house. That's it.

What you need: This printed kit, 6-8 players, drinks (strongly recommended), and one person willing to be the host (they can also play).

How It Works

  1. Hand out character cards (sealed — players read privately)
  2. Read the opening scene aloud
  3. Round 1: Introductions & alibis (20 min)
  4. Round 2: Clue discovery — send players to find hidden envelopes (20 min)
  5. Round 3: Accusations & interrogation (20 min)
  6. Final vote — everyone writes their guess
  7. Read the reveal script

📖 The Story

It's Saturday morning at Tidal Manor, a stunning beachfront rental on the Outer Banks. Eight friends reunited for a weekend getaway — their first time together since college. But when the group gathers for breakfast, Marcus Bellweather is found dead at the bottom of the widow's walk stairs.

The doors were locked from inside. The storm knocked out cell service. Nobody's leaving until the truth comes out.

The medical examiner (that's you, host) estimates time of death between 11 PM and 1 AM. Everyone claims they were asleep. Someone's lying.

👥 Characters

Sloane Whitfield
The Organizer — Marcus's ex-girlfriend
Booked the house. Still has Marcus's Netflix password. Claims she's "totally over it." Went to bed at 10 PM — alone.
🤫 SECRET: You saw Marcus arguing with Jack on the deck at 11:15 PM. You didn't tell anyone because you were supposed to be in your room.
Jack Reeves
The Best Friend — Marcus's college roommate
Loud, funny, life of the party. He and Marcus started a business together that failed. Says he was watching a movie in the den until midnight.
🤫 SECRET: Marcus owed you $40,000 from the failed business. You confronted him on the deck at 11 PM. It got heated. You shoved him. He walked away alive — you think.
Dr. Priya Nair
The Overachiever — the group's token success story
Surgeon. Calm under pressure. Was in the kitchen making tea at 11:30 PM. Heard nothing unusual.
🤫 SECRET: You're being sued for malpractice. Marcus found out and was going to tell the group — he thought it was "hilarious gossip." You needed him to stay quiet.
Tate Gallagher
The Drifter — showed up uninvited
Nobody's seen Tate in 3 years. Arrived Friday afternoon with a backpack and no explanation. Was "taking a walk on the beach" between 10 PM and midnight.
🤫 SECRET: You came to the reunion because Marcus had been blackmailing you. You have a criminal record nobody knows about. You needed it to stop.
Harper Quinn
The Social Media Star — 2M followers
Was live-streaming the sunset at 9 PM. Claims she took a sleeping pill and was out cold by 10:30. Her phone died — conveniently.
🤫 SECRET: Your "brand" is fake. Marcus found your burner account where you buy followers. He DMed you about it Thursday night. You deleted the messages.
Leo Chen
The Quiet One — Marcus's business partner (current)
Soft-spoken. Reads a lot. Was in the upstairs study "reading" from 9 PM onward. Nobody saw him until morning.
🤫 SECRET: You discovered Marcus was embezzling from your shared company. $200K missing. You confronted him at 12:30 AM on the widow's walk. He laughed at you.

🔍 Clue Packets

Hide these in envelopes around the house. Players find them during Round 2.

CLUE #1 — Found in the kitchen

A broken wine glass in the trash. Red wine stain on the counter. A napkin with writing: "Deck. 11 PM. We need to talk. —J"

CLUE #2 — Found under a couch cushion

Marcus's phone (cracked screen). Last text sent at 11:47 PM to an unsaved number: "I know what you did and I have proof. Meet me upstairs." Last text received at 11:52 PM: "On my way."

CLUE #3 — Found in the upstairs bathroom

A damp towel with a small bloodstain. A receipt from a hardware store dated Friday: "1x rope, 1x flashlight." Purchased with cash.

CLUE #4 — Found on the back deck

Scuff marks on the railing. A torn piece of fabric — dark blue, matches Leo's jacket. A financial document showing wire transfers from "Bellweather & Chen LLC" to an offshore account.

🎯 The Reveal

Read this after everyone votes:

The killer is Leo Chen.

Leo discovered Marcus was embezzling $200,000 from their company. He confronted Marcus on the widow's walk at 12:30 AM. Marcus laughed and said "What are you going to do about it?" Leo shoved him over the railing. The torn jacket fabric on the deck railing is Leo's. The hardware store receipt was Leo's — he bought the rope planning to stage it as a suicide but panicked and fled when Marcus fell.

Jack's argument was real but unrelated. Priya had motive but no opportunity (kitchen alibi confirmed by tea kettle timestamp on the smart home system). Tate was on the beach — wet sand on his shoes confirms it. Harper's phone actually died (battery confirmed at 0% by police later). Sloane saw the Jack argument but nothing after.

If your group guessed Leo — they win! 🎉
