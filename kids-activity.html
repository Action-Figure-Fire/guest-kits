🧠 Ocean Trivia

Ages 7+
Q1: What percentage of the Earth is covered by ocean?
A: About 71%!
Q2: What's the biggest animal in the ocean?
A: The blue whale — it's also the biggest animal that has EVER lived!
Q3: How much of the ocean has been explored?
A: Only about 5%! We know more about the moon.
Q4: Can jellyfish sting you after they're dead?
A: YES! Their tentacles can sting for hours after. Don't touch!
Q5: What do sea otters do when they sleep?
A: They hold hands so they don't drift apart. 🥺
Q6: What's the fastest fish in the ocean?
A: The sailfish — up to 68 mph!
Q7: How many arms does an octopus have?
A: 8 — but technically 6 are "arms" and 2 are "legs"!
Q8: What makes the ocean salty?
A: Rivers carry dissolved minerals and salts into the ocean over millions of years.
