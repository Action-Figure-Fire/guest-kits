Your Trip, Planned

Virginia Beach — 3-Day Itinerary

Three ways to spend your weekend. Pick your vibe. Mix and match. Or ignore it all — you're on vacation.

🏄 Adventure Itinerary

AdventureRelaxationFoodie
Day 1Beach & Boardwalk
8:00 AM
Sunrise beach walk — Hit the sand before the crowds. The stretch near 42nd Street is quietest.
9:30 AM
Breakfast at Commune — Fried chicken biscuit + cortado. Non-negotiable.
11:00 AM
Surf lesson — WRV Surf Camp at the oceanfront. 90 min. $65/person. Book ahead.
1:00 PM
Lunch at Waterman's — Orange crush + fish tacos on the patio.
3:00 PM
Bike the boardwalk — Rent cruisers at Cherie's. Ride from Rudee Inlet to Fort Story. 8 miles round trip.
6:00 PM
Sunset at First Landing State Park — 64th Street entrance. Bring a blanket.
8:00 PM
Dinner at Esoteric — The wagyu tartare and whatever cocktail they recommend.
Day 2Water & Wildlife
7:00 AM
Kayak tour — Back Bay Wildlife Refuge — Guided paddle through cypress swamps. Eagles, herons, turtles. Book at Ocean Eagle Kayak.
10:30 AM
Virginia Aquarium — Skip the crowds by going midmorning. The shark tank is worth it alone.
1:00 PM
Lunch at Chick's Oyster Bar — Raw bar + waterfront views. Casual. Cold beers.
3:00 PM
Jet ski rental — Rudee Inlet. 1 hour. $85. Pure adrenaline.
5:00 PM
Pool time — Decompress. You've earned it.
7:30 PM
Dinner at Catch 31 — Oceanfront. Splurge night. Get the lobster.
Day 3Explore & Depart
8:30 AM
Brunch at Doc Taylor's — Hole in the wall. Cash only. Best eggs Benedict at the beach.
10:30 AM
ViBe Creative District — Street art, local shops, galleries. Walk Cypress Ave + 19th Street.
12:00 PM
One last swim — Or a final walk on the beach. Take the photo you'll actually frame.
2:00 PM
Check out — Grab coffee at Three Ships for the road.

📝 Packing Reminder

☐ Sunscreen (reef-safe) · ☐ Water shoes · ☐ Beach towels · ☐ Cooler for the beach · ☐ Cash (some spots are cash-only) · ☐ Sunglasses · ☐ Light jacket for evenings · ☐ Phone charger/portable battery
