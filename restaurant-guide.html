🍽️ RESTAURANT GUIDE — SAMPLE

Insider Eats

Virginia Beach, VA — A Local's Guide

Skip the tourist traps. These are the places locals actually eat at. Curated by someone who's tried them all.

🔥 Must-Visit (Don't Miss These)

Esoteric $$$
New American / Craft Cocktails
★★★★★
"Speakeasy energy meets farm-to-table. The kind of place where the bartender remembers your name."

Small plates that hit way above their weight. Seasonal menu changes monthly. The cocktails are genuine art — not just Instagram bait.

🍴 Must Order

The Wagyu tartare, whatever seasonal cocktail they're pushing, and the duck fat fries. Trust the bartender's pick over the menu.

💡 Reservations required on weekends. Walk-ins easier Tue-Thu. Sit at the bar if you want the real experience.
Commune $$
Brunch / Coffee / All-Day Café
★★★★★
"The place where VB's creative class actually hangs out. If your Airbnb has a porch, get this to-go."

Best brunch in the city and it's not close. Their biscuits are legendary. Coffee program is serious — single origin, properly extracted.

🍴 Must Order

The fried chicken biscuit (get the hot honey), a cortado, and the seasonal grain bowl if you're pretending to be healthy.

💡 Saturday brunch line starts at 9:30. Get there at 9:00 or go on a weekday. Worth the wait regardless.
Waterman's Surfside Grille $$
Seafood / Beach Bar
★★★★☆
"The one tourist spot that actually deserves the hype. Because the orange crush is that good."

Yes, it's on every "best of VB" list. Yes, there's a wait. But the orange crush (fresh-squeezed OJ + vodka + triple sec) was literally invented here.

🍴 Must Order

The original orange crush. Fish tacos. She-crab soup if it's on the specials board.

🍺 Best Happy Hours

Yard House $$
Mon-Fri 3-6pm — Half-price apps + $2 off drafts

130+ beers on tap. The happy hour app menu is basically a full dinner. The mac & cheese bites and poke nachos are the move.

Whiskey Kitchen $$
Daily 4-7pm — $6 whiskey flights + half-price bar bites

500+ whiskeys. The flights let you try 3 for $6. Pair with their smoked wings. Dark wood, leather seats, feels like a proper whiskey bar.

🌅 Best Views While You Eat

1. Catch 31 — Hilltop area

Oceanfront fine dining. Sunset from the patio is unreal. Pricey but worth it for a special night.

2. Chick's Oyster Bar — Shore Drive

Casual. Waterfront. The raw bar is legit. Go at sunset, order a bucket of clams and a local beer.

3. Rudee's on the Inlet — Rudee Inlet

Watch the fishing boats come in while you eat what they caught yesterday. It doesn't get fresher.

🚫 Tourist Traps to Avoid

We won't name names, but: if it's on the main boardwalk, has a neon sign, and charges $18 for fish and chips — keep walking. The good stuff is 2-3 blocks inland or on Shore Drive.
