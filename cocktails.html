Pour House

Beach House Edition — 12 Recipes

Cocktails and mocktails designed for vacation mode. Simple ingredients, big flavors, zero bartending experience required.

🍸 The Cocktails

The VB Crush
Cocktail

Born on the Virginia Beach boardwalk. The drink that starts every vacation.

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • Juice of 2 fresh oranges
  • Splash of lemon-lime soda
  • Orange slice for garnish
  1. Fill a pint glass with ice
  2. Add vodka and triple sec
  3. Squeeze fresh oranges directly into the glass (this is non-negotiable — no bottled OJ)
  4. Top with a splash of soda
  5. Stir gently. Garnish. Done.
🍴 Pairs with: Fish tacos, shrimp, anything from the grill
Dark & Stormy
Cocktail

Two ingredients. Zero skill required. Tastes like you spent a semester in Bermuda.

  • 2 oz dark rum (Gosling's if you can find it)
  • 4 oz ginger beer (not ginger ale — big difference)
  • Lime wedge
  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Pour ginger beer
  3. Float the dark rum on top (pour slowly over the back of a spoon)
  4. Squeeze lime. Don't stir — let it storm.
🍴 Pairs with: Jerk chicken, grilled pineapple, Caribbean anything
Frozen Mango Margarita
Cocktail

Blender required. Dignity optional.

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 0.5 oz agave syrup
  • Tajín for the rim
  1. Rim glass with lime + Tajín
  2. Blend everything with 1 cup ice until smooth
  3. Pour. Add a paper umbrella if available. Mandatory.
Aperol Sunset
Cocktail

The official drink of "golden hour on the porch." Light, bubbly, dangerously drinkable.

  • 3 oz prosecco
  • 2 oz Aperol
  • 1 oz soda water
  • Orange slice
  1. Fill a wine glass with ice
  2. Add prosecco, then Aperol
  3. Top with soda. Garnish with orange.
  4. Drink while watching the sun go down. This is the rule.

🥤 The Mocktails

Watermelon Cooler
Mocktail
  • 2 cups fresh watermelon, cubed
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Sparkling water
  • Mint leaves
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Blend watermelon until smooth. Strain if you want.
  2. Pour over ice. Add lime juice and a pinch of salt.
  3. Top with sparkling water. Slap the mint (releases oils) and drop it in.
Ginger Lemonade Smash
Mocktail
  • 2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1 oz honey
  • 4 oz ginger beer
  • Rosemary sprig
  1. Dissolve honey in lemon juice (microwave 10 sec if needed)
  2. Pour over ice. Top with ginger beer.
  3. Bruise the rosemary between your palms and drop it in. Looks fancy. Tastes incredible.
