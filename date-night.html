💬 Conversation Cards

Take turns drawing a card. Both answer. No phones. No "I don't know." Go deep.

Light & Fun
If we could move anywhere in the world tomorrow — no job constraints — where would you pick and why?
Light & Fun
What's a hobby you've always wanted to try but felt too embarrassed to start?
Deeper
What's something I do that makes you feel most loved — that I might not even realize?
Deeper
What's a fear you've never told anyone? Not a phobia — a real, deep fear about life.
Spicy
If you could relive one moment in our relationship — exactly as it happened — which would you pick?
Spicy
What did you think of me the very first time we met? Be honest. I can handle it.
Future
Describe our perfect ordinary Tuesday five years from now. Where are we? What does the day look like?
Future
What's one thing you want us to accomplish together in the next year?
Wild Card
If a movie was made about us, what genre would it be and who plays each of us?
Wild Card
What's the most ridiculous argument we've ever had? Can we laugh about it now?

🍷 Wine Tasting Scorecard

Grab 3 bottles (or whatever you've got). Pour small amounts. Rate blind if you're feeling brave.

1
Wine: _______________
Look ___ / Smell ___ / Taste ___ / Overall ___ (each out of 5)
2
Wine: _______________
Look ___ / Smell ___ / Taste ___ / Overall ___ (each out of 5)
3
Wine: _______________
Look ___ / Smell ___ / Taste ___ / Overall ___ (each out of 5)

Tasting tips: Look at the color against a white background. Swirl and sniff before tasting. First sip = first impression. Second sip = what you actually think. Disagree loudly.
