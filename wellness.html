🌅 Morning Routine (20 min)

The Vacation Wake-Up

Do this before coffee. Yes, really.
1
Breathwork — Box Breathing (2 min)
Inhale 4 counts → Hold 4 counts → Exhale 4 counts → Hold 4 counts. Repeat 8 times. Resets your nervous system.
2
Gratitude (2 min)
Before you check your phone: name 3 things you're grateful for right now. Say them out loud. Feels weird. Works anyway.
3
Cold Water Face Splash (30 sec)
Cold water on your face activates the mammalian dive reflex — drops heart rate, increases alertness. Free cold plunge for your face.
4
Sun Exposure (5 min)
Step outside. Look toward the sun (not at it). Morning light sets your circadian clock, boosts cortisol awakening response, and improves sleep that night.
5
Movement Flow (10 min)
See the stretching sequence below. Do it barefoot on the porch or beach if possible. Grounding = free electrons from the earth.

🤸 10-Minute Stretch Flow

1
Cat-Cow (1 min)
On all fours. Inhale = arch back, look up. Exhale = round spine, tuck chin. Slow. Wake up the spine.
2
Downward Dog (1 min)
Push hips up and back. Pedal the feet. Let your head hang. Stretches hamstrings, calves, shoulders.
3
Low Lunge — each side (2 min total)
Step one foot forward, drop back knee. Sink hips. Raise arms overhead. Opens hip flexors — crucial if you've been sitting in a car.
4
Standing Forward Fold (1 min)
Feet hip-width. Fold at the hips. Grab opposite elbows and sway. Let gravity do the work. Don't force it.
5
Spinal Twist — each side (2 min total)
Lie on back. Knees to chest, drop both to one side. Arms out in T. Look opposite direction. Best stretch for travel-stiff backs.
6
Child's Pose (1 min)
Knees wide, big toes touching, arms extended. Breathe into your back. This is where you remember you're on vacation.
7
Savasana (2 min)
Lie flat. Eyes closed. Palms up. Do absolutely nothing. You're not being lazy — you're integrating.

🧘 Guided Meditation Prompts

No app needed. Read one slowly, then close your eyes for 5-10 minutes.

🌊 Ocean Breath

Close your eyes. Listen to the waves — or imagine them. Match your breathing to the rhythm. Inhale as the wave builds. Exhale as it crashes. You are not separate from the ocean. You are 60% water. You belong here. Stay for as long as feels right.

☀️ Body Scan

Start at the top of your head. Notice any tension. Don't fix it — just notice. Move down: forehead, jaw (unclench it), shoulders (drop them), chest, belly, hips, legs, feet. Wherever you find tension, breathe into it. Your body has been carrying things. Give it permission to set them down.

🥾 Local Trails

First Landing State Park — Bald Cypress Trail
1.5 miles · Flat · Easy
Boardwalk through a cypress swamp. Spanish moss, turtles, and silence. The most peaceful 45 minutes in Virginia Beach.
Back Bay National Wildlife Refuge — Bay Trail
4 miles · Flat/Sandy · Moderate
Dunes, maritime forest, and a pristine bay. Bring binoculars — osprey, eagles, and fox sightings are common.
False Cape State Park — Barbour Hill Trail
6 miles · Sand/Trail · Challenging
Remote. No cars. You have to hike or bike in. Deserted beach, wild horses (sometimes), and total solitude. Pack water.
